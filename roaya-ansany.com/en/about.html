Roaya Humanitarian Foundation

Roaya is a non-profit humanitarian organization that provides life-saving assistance to the poor, orphans, vulnerable people, and the displaced in areas affected by conflicts, wars, and disasters. The foundation funds its projects through donations from generous supporters.

We strive to extend a helping hand to those in need and those affected

wherever they are

Vision & Goals

We strive to build a more compassionate and just society by enabling individuals and organizations to contribute to humanitarian work, and by achieving sustainable development through projects that meet people’s needs across different fields.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading charitable organizations in relief and humanitarian aid, contributing to a world of solidarity and mercy where every person enjoys a dignified life regardless of their circumstances.

Our Mission

We seek to extend a helping hand to those in need and those affected wherever they are, and to alleviate their suffering through sustainable humanitarian projects that reinforce solidarity and mercy. We work with transparency and high efficiency to ensure donations reach those who deserve them and to create real positive impact that restores hope.

Where we work

Roaya Humanitarian Foundation

We operate in the humanitarian and charitable field, providing life-saving services to the poor, orphans, vulnerable people, and the displaced in areas affected by conflicts, wars, and disasters. We currently work in several countries: Palestine, Lebanon, Syria, Sudan, and to serve the poor and those in need in Türkiye. Our activities rely entirely on donations.

We have implemented 24,537 projects in 5 different countries, benefiting more than 8.2 million people in need.
