Campaign details

In response to the needs of affected families, Roaya Humanitarian Foundation implemented this project with the aim of enabling families to meet their needs according to their priorities.

This type of support is based on providing families with the flexibility to choose what they need, whether it be food, medicine, or other daily necessities, in a way that suits each family’s circumstances.

This project comes as a response to the varying needs among families, as a single solution cannot address all challenges amid the diversity of hardships they face.

Roaya Humanitarian Foundation continues to implement such initiatives, contributing to supporting families and empowering them to better cope with their circumstances

Royaa is a giving platform dedicated to people who care about the real impact of their giving.
