The Palestinian Scholars’ League affirms Roaya Humanitarian Foundation’s commitment to transparency and Sharia-compliant standards in the collection and distribution of Zakat funds.

Palestinian Scholars’ League Commends Roaya Humanitarian Foundation

The Palestinian Scholars’ League issued an official statement affirming that Zakat is one of the greatest pillars of Islam and a sacred trust that must be managed in accordance with the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and adherence to Sharia principles. In this context, the League announced its endorsement of Roaya Humanitarian Foundation – Turkey, praising its role in managing Zakat funds in a responsible and disciplined manner in line with Islamic guidelines. The League noted that the Foundation has demonstrated a clear commitment to ensuring that Zakat is collected and distributed to its rightful beneficiaries in full compliance with Islamic law. The League further emphasized that Roaya Humanitarian Foundation adopts precise mechanisms to identify eligible beneficiaries, while ensuring that assistance is delivered in a timely manner and in a way that preserves the dignity of recipients. It also highlighted that the Foundation’s approach reflects a high level of transparency and accountability, along with a strong sense of trustworthiness in fulfilling this religious responsibility. Additionally, the League stated that the Foundation’s Zakat programs demonstrate professionalism in humanitarian work, ethical governance, and genuine care for the most vulnerable groups, contributing to alleviating their suffering and strengthening social solidarity. In conclusion, the Palestinian Scholars’ League affirmed that Roaya Humanitarian Foundation – Turkey is a reliable entity for managing Zakat funds, recommending it to donors and partners as a safe and trustworthy channel for fulfilling the obligation of Zakat in accordance with Sharia principles.

A brief overview.

Roaya Humanitarian Organization

Roaya Humanitarian Foundation is a non-profit civil organization operating within the framework of Turkish laws. It is dedicated to providing humanitarian and relief services to the most vulnerable groups, with a primary focus on supporting affected and needy families in Turkey, as well as vulnerable communities in Syria and Palestine. The Foundation operates through a professional approach grounded in core humanitarian principles, with a clear commitment to transparency, accountability, and respect for human dignity at every stage of its work. Roaya Humanitarian Foundation strives to serve as a platform that bridges social responsibility with structured institutional action, by developing programs that respond to urgent needs, strengthen community resilience, and deliver relief and development solutions that contribute to improving the quality of life of beneficiaries. The Foundation carries out its work through a specialized team with strong field and administrative expertise, operating under a governance framework that ensures service quality, operational safety, and full alignment with internationally recognized humanitarian standards. In addition, the Foundation relies on a broad network of local and international partnerships that enable it to implement higher-impact projects, expand the scope of its response, and deliver professional and sustainable interventions. Through this role, Roaya Humanitarian Foundation seeks to be an active contributor to humanitarian efforts aimed at protecting vulnerable populations, fostering solidarity, and helping build a safer and more equitable future for those in need of support.

The implementation of these programs relies on regular needs assessments, a broad network of local and international partnerships, and methodologies aligned with humanitarian standards.
